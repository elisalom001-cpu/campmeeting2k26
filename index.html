Dr. Seneque Edmond is the current president of the Adventist University of Haiti (UNAH). He is an inspiring speaker and dedicated leader known for his impactful messages on faith, family, and spiritual growth.

All churches are invited to participate in the Camp Meeting 2026 Gift Exchange. This activity is intended to encourage interaction, fellowship, and familiarity among participants. Each registered participant should prepare a gift with a minimum value of $20.
